_Have your work shown in an exhibition running at the Riverside Museum at Blake's Lock, 28th-30th April! We are running a competition to gather eleven great pieces of work that interpret the local area and local art._  


  
[http://www.remixreading.org/artcomp](http://www.remixreading.org/artcomp)  


  
Work must be CC licensed, in traditional media or hardcopy, and may be remixed from existing CC images of the area around the town of Reading (in England).
